Section What happened
Government In our government, we elected people to each role required to run a city, county, and state government.
State Patrol The state patrol learned what the Iowa State troopers do on a daily basis. These are the boys that walked around in the evenings arresting people if they didn't have their ID's.
Law School The lawyers learned about the law. They were given former cases and had mock trials, learning about the proceedings in a court room.
